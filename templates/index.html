Mission

We aim to serve as an all-in-one resource page for homeless individuals in Boston. Our goal is to create a cohesive community of support for these individuals no matter where they are in the city.

Project

Through an up-to-date map of resources in Boston, we provide individuals with an understanding of the support near them. The resources mapped out are currently in 3 categories: shelters, food, and skill training facilities. We also allow for shelters to log in to our website, in order to dynamically update the resources that they provide on the site as well as hotline numbers and capacity limits.
